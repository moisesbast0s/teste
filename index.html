Soccer

Introduction

Soccer, also known as football in many countries, is a popular sport played by two teams of eleven players with a spherical ball. It is played by 250 million players in over 200 countries, making it the world's most popular sport.

Rules

The game is played on a rectangular field with a goal at each end. The objective of the game is to score by getting the ball into the opposing goal. Players are not allowed to touch the ball with their hands or arms while it is in play, except for the goalkeepers within the penalty area.
